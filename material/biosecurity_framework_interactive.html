This interactive diagram illustrates the integrated framework of biosecurity and cybersecurity in global logistics systems. Cybersecurity plays a central role in enabling and protecting biosecurity functions across all domains, from agricultural biosecurity to laboratory biosafety. The framework emphasizes two critical domains: Logistics (including truck, marine transport, and container security) and Ports Management (covering operational impacts, current models, and cyber tools with satellite data).
